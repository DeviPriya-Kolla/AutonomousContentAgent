Autonomous Content Agent

Your automated social media content discovery and drafting engine.

This application demonstrates a fully autonomous AI agent that constantly works in the background. It monitors news sources, intelligently decides what's important, and drafts on-brand content for social media.

Key Capabilities:

  • Monitors multiple RSS feeds 24/7.
  • Uses AI to filter for relevant, significant news.
  • Scrapes articles for context before drafting.
  • Generates content in a specific, user-defined brand voice.
